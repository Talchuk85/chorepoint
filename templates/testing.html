chorepoint is a digital motivation app.

  • this app is for anyone who needs a little extra push to get things done. create tasks and give them a point value. then, create rewards and set how much they're worth.

  • once you have earned the points by getting stuff done, treat yourself to some sweet rewards!
  • for more fun, manage someone else's tasks and rewards within a home. they'll be able to submit tasks for you to approve, and you'll approve the rewards as well! you can also manually add or subtract points at your discretion.

    • let's get motivated!

    created with flask and mysql.
